About SmartTo-DoList

SmartTo-DoList is a modern web application for managing tasks and reminders, using artificial intelligence. Our application helps you organize your work, studies, and personal affairs, without missing anything important.

With smart features, you can quickly add tasks, set priorities, get timely reminders, and sync everything with your Telegram bot.

🧠 Smart Features

🎯 Smart Reminders

The system automatically determines the priority of your tasks and reminds you of them at the right time.

📱 Telegram Sync

Get task notifications right in Telegram and manage them from the messenger.

🗣️ Speech Recognition

Add tasks by voice! Just say what you need to do, and the neural network will create a task.

⏰ Fuzzy Deadlines

Specify deadlines in natural language: "next week", "on Friday", "in 3 days".

📖 Instructions

Telegram Sync

  1. Open Telegram and find the bot @SmartToDo_bot
  2. Press "Start" (/start)
  3. Link your account using the code from the "Settings" section in the application
